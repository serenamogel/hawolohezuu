Jade Cargill Stays In Touch With Tony Khan, Theres No Place Shed Rather Be Than AEW

March 18, 2024
blog

Jade Cargill believes nothing will stop her from becoming a household name in the world of professional wrestling.

All Elite Wrestling‘s Jade Cargill recently sat down with Women’s Wrestling Talk. When asked about fans anticipating her return to All Elite Wrestling, Cargill put over her fanbase and said it feels phenomenal that they want her back.

“Hate me or love me, I’m still making money. I can care less,” Jade Cargill said. “I love our fans. My fan base is the best. The Jade Cargill fan base is the best. That’s undeniable. I started out with a great. I’m surrounded with nothing but greats and the greats in the wrestling community want me to succeed.

“There’s nothing that’s gonna stop me from becoming nothing but a household name and a great. So it feels phenomenal that our fans want me back. I mean, why wouldn’t they? Who else is Jade Cargill? Nobody.”

Jade Cargill: Tony Khan is a phenomenal boss

When asked if she’s still in contact with Tony Khan, Cargill says she always is and puts over how great of a boss he is.

“I am. Always. I mean, he’s a phenomenal boss. I could hit him up now. He’ll probably hit me back up in three minutes. This is a man who has four companies, a professional soccer team, Jacksonville Jaguars; he’s busy. He literally comes to every show.

“He is so passionate; he loves our company. There’s no other place I’d rather be. He doesn’t look at me as a number; he looks at me as a human being. He knows my journey; he understands my purpose in the wrestling community and what I want to achieve. And I don’t think you can beat that.”
