8 Things You Didnt Know About Ali Asghar

June 28, 2024

Indian actor and stand-up comedian, Ali Asghar is famous for his classy acting and comic skills that make him unique among all. The actor has been giving his best to the film and television industry as he has worked on various projects so far and got much acclaim during his career life. The actor also appeared in ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ as amused the audience with his tremendous comic skills. Also, he was a part of the popular Indian television serial ‘Kahani Ghar Ghar Ki’ as Kamal Agarwal which became his identity at that time as it was the reason for his fame. Additionally, he took part in various comedy shows including ‘Comedy Circus 1’ with Kashif Khan and ‘Comedy Circus Ke Taansen’ with Kapil Sharma, where he emerged as a winner twice, consequently, he then got a chance to appear in Comedy Nights with Kapil where he appeared as Dadi. Well, he has got a lot of fame and recognition during his career life. Here are some more facts you probably did not know about.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Ali Ashar

1- The Cutest Kid

The famous actor, Ali Asghar was born on 25th July 1966 in India. He did graduation in the field of Political Science, and later completed his higher studies in the field of Hotel Management. While he took the start of his acting career as a child artist in grade 10th.

2- Put his Career at stake

After doing Masters, the most talented guy got the chance to work for a hotel which was located abroad but he refused and stayed back with his family.

3- Happy Family

The actor married his long-term love lady Siddika Asghar and the couple is blessed with two cute kids- a son named Nuyaan and a daughter named Ada.

4- True Talent

The most talented guy, Asghar has done various programs including television serials, and comedy shows, like; Comedy Circus 1 with Kashif Khan and Comedy Circus Ke Taansen with Kapil Sharma where he emerged as the winner and recognized as a true talent.

5- Multi-talented Star

The actor worked in many Bollywood films including ‘Josh’ starring Shahrukh Khan, Priya Gill, and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, ‘Shadi No.1’, ‘Tees Maar Khan, and many more for which he got recognition.

6- Success at Peak

Besides doing a lot of projects including television and film projects, the actor never stops struggling and continues to enthrall the millions of hearts with his tremendous performances.

7- Tragic Phase

There was a tragic phase in his life when an FIR was filed against him for allegedly hurting religious feelings by mimicking Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim in Comedy Nights with Kapil.

8- Rose to fame

Ali Asghar rose to fame after appearing as Kamal Agarwal in the top-rated television serial ‘Kahani Ghar Ghar Ki’ which was aired on Star Plus and got much popularity.
