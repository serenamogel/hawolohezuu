➡ Sid Makkar Biography

Sid Makkar is an actor, works as a film & television actor. He has also worked in the Bollywood film industry and played a lead role in movies including “Dus TOola”, “Lucky By Chance” and others. Also, he has made his career in theatre plays, films, and television as well for which he got immense love and respect from his fans across the globe. There are more things to know about him and we are presenting some information related to his life.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Sid Makkar

His Birth & Family Background

He was born on 15th May, Amritsar, Punjab, India, belongs to a Hindu family. On 2nd December 2007, he married to Shalini, who is an air hostess by profession.

His Career In Theatre

He made his career in theatre as he has worked in a number of plays in Hindi and English as well as he played the title role in the musical Zangoora at the Kingdom of Dreams.

His Film Career

He has played the lead roles in Bollywood films including “Dus Tola, “Lucky By chance”, “Stright” released in 2009, and others.

He Appeared In Television Serials

He appeared in Indian television serials and is best known for his role as Vidyut in ” Yeh Hain Mohbaten” aired in 2016-2017.

He Is Best Known For

He is best known for the “State of Siege: 26/11”, released in (2020), “Sense8” released in (2015), and “The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel”, released in (2011).

He Is An Award-Winning Actor

He has won the “Zee Rishtey Award” for the category of “Favourite Nayi Jodi”.

He Made His Bollywood Debut With

He has acted in various films but has made debut with “Kis Kis Ki Kismat ” which was released in the year 2014. He then offered many roles in Bollywood movies.

He Made His Television Debut

He acted in many serials and made television debut with the Indian serial named “Ghar Ki Lakshmi Betiyaan” aired in the year 2006.
