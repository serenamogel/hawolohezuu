Best Cranberry Juice, According to Our Taste Tests

June 16, 2024
blog

The best cranberry juice is the perfect balance of tartness and sweetness. It’s great on its own or mixed into a cocktail. No matter how good your cranberry juice is, though, I’m sorry to say it probably won’t cure your UTI. That’s a myth. Please go to the doctor.

If you’re drinking it for pleasure, however, this is a list for you. Whether you have a child who demands their juice fix or you want to step up your Vodka Cran game, we here at Sporked have tried all the nationally available, easily accessible cranberry juices on the market in two separate taste tests—and we’re ready to report back. From the best cranberry juice without sugar (yep, get ready to scrunch up your face) to the best cranberry juice to drink straight out of a glass, here’s our berry-special list of the seven best cranberry juice brands (unsweetened cranberry juice and diet cranberry juice, too).

This diet cranberry juice, on the other hand, is some of the best cranberry juice we tried. It’s actually very tasty; it tastes less like artificial sweeteners than other diet cranberry juices. Ocean Spray’s diet cranberry juice is better than some non-diet versions of other cranberry juices on the market. You can always bet on Ocean Spray! I know this because I spent a full year giving out free samples of pure, unsweetened cranberry juice in grocery stores when I was in college. I would warn people that it was very strong and not sweet at all, and they would still proceed to grab a tiny cup, chug it down, and then choke on the bitterness. Then they’d yell at me for giving them something that tastes bad. So please don’t yell at me if you try this and don’t like it! It doesn’t taste good! But if you’re looking for pure cranberry juice for health benefits, this is the best cranberry juice we tasted. Trust me, I’m an actual expert on this. —Jordan Myrick

If you’re splurging on the expensive vodka, why not save a few bucks on your cran? Best Bulk

Kirkland Signature Ocean Spray Cranberry JuiceCostco

Fun fact: Kirkland Signature Cranberry Juice is actually made by Ocean Spray, and yes, you can taste the similarities. Both source cranberries from same North American farmers and include most of the same ingredients, with one key difference—Ocean Spray (spoiler: our #1 pick for the best cranberry juice) uses a juice blend of cranberry, grape, apple, and pear. Kirkland (aka Costco’s house brand) skips the pear juice entirely and sticks with those first three juices instead. The result is a deep, robust flavor that’s incredibly hard to fault. It’s tart, bitter, and lightly sweet on the finish, with no added sugars or preservatives. The only downside here is that you have to buy two at a time. (Though, when you’re buying some of the best cranberry juice on the market, I’m not sure that’s a downside.)  —Ariana Losch
