Cheyenne Jackson playing Tom Sandoval is so spot-on and hilarious I had to do a double-take

January 21, 2024
blog

Cheyenne Jackson plays Tom Sandoval in a parody of the Vanderpump Rules finale, hosted by Andy Cohen and Radio Andy Theater. Plus, Chrissy Teigen is playing Bravo queen Lisa Vanderpump.

Known for his Broadway musicals, Cheyenne took a dive into the world of reality TV by playing Tom Sandoval. The Vanderpump Rules finale was too good to not do a parody, and we’re officially obsessed.

Cheyenne Jackson as Tom Sandoval

Broadway actor Cheyenne Jackson plays Tom Sandoval in the Vanderpump Rules finale recreation. The whole moustache situation paired with the beanie is absolutely giving *Scandoval season*.

He’s fully gone into Sandoval mode for the Radio Andy Theater reading, and even fake-cries from when Tom sparked tears in the finale. Cheyenne is currently playing Sir Harry in Once Upon a Mattress.

Cheyenne has played parts in American Horror Story and Descendants 3, but this is his first delve into the reality TV world, playing Sandoval just perfectly at the Green Room 42 in New York City on Monday.

Vanderpump Rules finale turned into parody

It wasn’t just Cheyenne portraying Tom that was iconic, but Chrissy Teigen played Lisa Vanderpump, and Big Brother’s Carson Kressley took on James Kennedy.

Teigen also got the crowd going while doing her best to play Vanderpump with her famous British accent fans love, but she wasn’t afraid to poke fun at herself for not always nailing it.

Cohen and SiriusXM organized the event to promote the upcoming season 11 premiere of Vanderpump Rules, which began filming just a few months after Sandoval and Raquel Leviss’ affair was discovered.
