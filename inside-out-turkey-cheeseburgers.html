    The BEST Turkey Burgers

    Fire up that grill and throw on these Inside Out Turkey Cheeseburgers! Loaded with flavor and filled with fresh mozzarella cheese, green onions, and Dijon mustard - these easy burgers are going to light up your 4th of July celebration!

    Course Entree Cuisine American Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Cook Time 10 minutes minutes Total Time 20 minutes minutes Servings 4 burgers Calories 450kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 4 oz fresh mozzarella
    • 1 lb ground turkey
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
    • 1/4 cup chopped green onions about 3 green onions
    • 1 egg
    • black pepper
    • 4 hamburger buns
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 4 Boston lettuce leaves
    • 4 large slices tomatoes

    Instructions

    • Dice mozzarella into Â½-inch (or slightly smaller) cubes.

    • Combine turkey, cheese, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, green onions and egg in a large bowl. Season with black pepper.

    • Using your hands, combine all ingredients in the bowl and form into 4 patties.

    • Heat grill to medium high heat and oil grates.

    • Grill burgers until cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes on each side.

    • Warm bun halves on the grill until lightly toasted.

    • Spread the bottom of each bun with 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise. Top with 1 lettuce leave, 1 large slice tomato, turkey burger and top bun.

    • Enjoy!
