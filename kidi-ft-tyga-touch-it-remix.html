Dennis Nana Dwamena, better known by his stage name KiDi kicks off 2022 in a grand style with the release of the remix to his chart topping tune, “Touch It“.

The Lynx Entertainment superstar, KiDi a.k.a Gyal Dem Sugar connects with highly rated American rap artiste, Micheal Ray Stevenson, professionally called Tyga to unlock this potential hit single.

The original record, ‘Touch It’ happens to be track three off KiDi’s thirteen tracked studio album, ‘The Golden Boy

‘ which was released in June 2021. The project has received positive comments and reviews from his listeners.
