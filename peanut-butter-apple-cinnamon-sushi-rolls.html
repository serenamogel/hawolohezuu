Peanut Butter Apple Cinnamon Sushi Rolls

Peanut Butter Apple Cinnamon Sushi Rolls are the perfect snack or lunch for kids!

Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword peanut butter apple cinnamon sushi rolls Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Total Time 10 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 107kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread crusts removed (makes rolling easier)
  • 2 to 3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 apple cut into sticks
  • 2 tsp honey
  • cinnamon a sprinkle

Instructions

  • Spread the peanut butter onto the bread in a thin, even layer.

  • Place the apple sticks on top.

  • Drizzle the honey on top of the apples and sprinkle with cinnamon.

  • Slowly roll the the bread up (like a cinnamon roll) making sure to press firmly so the bread sticks together.

  • Slice each oll into four pieces and serve.
