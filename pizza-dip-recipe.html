    What You’ll Need

    Only a few simple ingredients are required for this easy hot dip recipe! As always, you can find the full printable recipe with complete instructions in the recipe card at the end of this post. Let’s take a look at what you’ll need:

    • cream cheese – you’ll want to make sure your cream cheese is fully softened so it combines with the other ingredients and spreads easily. I do recommend full fat cream cheese.
    • sour cream – or plain greek yogurt or even mayonnaise will work in this recipe.
    • Italian seasoning – I use this ingredient twice in this recipe for really great flavor. Use homemade Italian seasoning or store bought.
    • pizza sauce – you can also use your favorite marinara sauce in this recipe.
    • mozzarella cheese – I highly recommend using block cheese that you shred yourself for the best meltability.
    • Pepperoni slices – you can use regular sized pepperoni or mini pepperonis 
    • Parmesan cheese – either fresh or jarred cheese will work.

    Serve this dip with crackers, corn chips, tortilla chips, crostini, garlic bread, or vegetables. 

    How To Make Pizza Dip Recipe

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F. Lightly spray a baking dish.
  • Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and Italian seasoning. Spread into the dish.
  • Top with the pizza sauce (or marinara sauce if you are using that).
  • Add the mozzarella cheese and top with pepperoni slices.
  • Bake for 15 to 20 minutes. If you prefer, you can broil the dip for 2-3 minutes to brown the cheese slightly.
  • Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Serve warm with your choice of dippers

    • If your pepperoni is greasy after baking you can use a paper towel to gently blot the pepperoni and soak up the grease. 

    Storage Information

    Store any leftover Pizza Dip tightly covered in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. I don’t recommend freezing this dip.

    FAQs

    How do you reheat Pizza Dip?

    You can reheat this dip either in the microwave or the oven on low heat. If reheating in the oven I recommend heating at 325°F and covering with foil or an oven-safe lid to prevent the cheese from over-browning. 

    What other toppings can I add to this dip?

    You can add any toppings to this dip that you enjoy on your pizza. Cooked sausage, black olives, peppers, onions, mushrooms, ham, ground beef, or even pineapple are all great options. 

    Can I freeze this dip?

    I don’t recommend it. Freezing dairy can be tricky and the texture will not be ideal.

    Trish’s Tips and Tricks

    This recipe is delicious and easy but check out my favorite tips and tricks for more information for the best results:

    • Use room temperature cream cheese that is in block from, not a tub, for the best results.
    • Make sure to use a good quality pizza sauce or make your own for the best flavor.
    • Add your favorite pizza toppings for even more flavor. Try Italian sausage, mushrooms, peppers, etc.

    Pizza Dip Recipe

    This easy Pizza Dip recipe is the perfect cheesy appetizer for your next party! This hot pepperoni pizza dip is so easy to make and tastes like a pizza without the crust! Serve with your choice of dippers for a delicious appetizer everyone will love. Perfect for parties, game days and more!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword pepperoni pizza dip, pizza dip, pizza dip recipe Prep Time 15 minutes minutes Cook Time 20 minutes minutes Servings 8 Calories 262kcal Author Trish – Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 ounces cream cheese softened (226 grams)
    • ⅔ cup sour cream or plain greek yogurt or mayonnaise (164 grams)
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    • 1 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce (256 grams)
    • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese about 2 cups, divided (226 grams)
    • 15 Pepperoni slices you'll need more if you're using the mini Pepperoni

    Instructions

    • Preheat your oven to 350°F. Lightly spray a 9 inch pie plate or 7 x 11 inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray and set aside.

    • In a mixing bowl combine the cream cheese, sour cream, and 1 teaspoon of the Italian seasoning. Spread the mixture into the prepared pie plate.

      8 ounces cream cheese, ⅔ cup sour cream, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

    • Top the cream cheese mixture with the pizza sauce (or marinara sauce if you are using that).

      1 cup pizza sauce

    • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the pizza sauce and top with your desired amount of pepperoni slices.

      8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, 15 Pepperoni slices

    • Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted. If you prefer, you can broil the dip for 2-3 minutes to brown the cheese slightly.

    • Sprinkle with the remaining ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning and grated Parmesan cheese.

      ⅓ cup Parmesan cheese, ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning

    • Serve warm with your choice of dippers.

    Notes

    Serve this dip with crackers, corn chips, tortilla chips, crostini, garlic bread, or vegetables.
    Storage Information: Store any leftover Pizza Dip tightly covered in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. I don’t recommend freezing this dip. You can reheat this dip either in the microwave or the oven on low heat. If reheating in the oven I recommend heating at 325°F and covering with foil or an oven-safe lid to prevent the cheese from over-browning.
